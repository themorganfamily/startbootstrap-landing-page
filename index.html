What's Happening?

Food, Fun, Stalls and an Auction for the Morgan family.




DOORALONG PUBLIC HALL

SUNDAY 4th DECEMBER
12:30pm - 5:30pm

Why?

Brett Morgan is a loving father of 5 school age children and supportive husband to his wife Cecile. Recently Brett has been diagnosed with serious brain cancer- he is in the middle of major treatment that means he won’t be able to earn an income for more than 6 months.

Brett has always been someone to help others, now we want to help him and the family.

A Local Family


Brett and Cecile have lived at Jilliby for nearly ten years and Brett’s family have been in the Dooralong Valley for nearly 70 years. They are part of the Valley family.

The Valley communities are coming together to support Brett and Cecile, so that he can have a bit more peace of mind to help him focus on getting the best result from the treatment.
